Waitlist One purpose

Take a seat.

Seats open quietly, in small groups. Leave your address and a sentence about your business. We’ll write to you when there’s a seat — not before, and not because we want to.

We don’t send marketing emails in the meantime.

what next

What happens after you submit.

  1. Within minutes

    A short confirmation email.

    From a real address. No no-reply. If you reply, a person reads it.

  2. When a seat opens

    A second email.

    It tells you what tier is open, what your first season would look like, and what we’d need from you to start. You decide from there.

  3. Until then

    Silence, on purpose.

    No newsletter, no “just checking in” email, no growth-marketing drip. We don’t do those, even to friends.
