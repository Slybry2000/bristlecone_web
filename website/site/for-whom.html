§ III Who it’s for Self-selection, in two columns

Find your column.

We’d rather you spend ninety seconds reading these two lists than ninety minutes on a sales call you didn’t need.

If you are…

The right reader.

  • A solo operator or small team running a business you intend to keep for decades.
  • A trades business, professional service, craft maker, or long-tail SaaS.
  • Someone who has been burned by aggressive marketing tools and growth-hackers.
  • Comfortable approving a draft email before it sends.
  • Skeptical of “AI does it all” and prefer your hand on the wheel.
  • Patient. Or willing to become patient.
  • Allergic to dashboards that exist to be looked at, not used.
If you are…

The wrong reader.

  • A funded startup with hockey-stick KPIs.
  • Looking for fully autonomous marketing automation.
  • In a hurry to triple revenue this quarter.
  • Allergic to reading drafts before they ship.
  • Convinced “the AI” should write your strategy without your input.
  • Measuring success in launches per quarter.
  • Looking for the “set and forget” promise.

Bristlecone is built for the first column. If you’re in the second, we wish you well — and we mean it. There are excellent tools out there for that job.
