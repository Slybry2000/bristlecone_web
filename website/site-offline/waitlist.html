Waitlist One purpose

Take a seat.

Seats open quietly, in small groups. We’re not running a submission form yet — we’d rather not pretend to collect what we can’t yet hold. Write to us directly. A person reads it. We’ll write back when there’s a seat — not before, and not because we want to.

Write to us →

By writing to us, you consent to our Privacy Policy. We use your name, email, and what you sent only to write to you when there’s a seat. Not before.

what next

What happens after you write.

  1. Within minutes

    A short confirmation email.

    From a real address. No no-reply. If you reply, a person reads it.

  2. When a seat opens

    A second email.

    It tells you what tier is open, what your first season would look like, and what we’d need from you to start. You decide from there.

  3. Until then

    Silence, on purpose.

    No newsletter, no “just checking in” email, no growth-marketing drip. We don’t do those, even to friends.
