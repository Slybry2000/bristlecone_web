Display: EB Garamond, an old-style serif with letterpress character. Body: Lora, a refined humanist serif. No tech-startup sans, no display gimmicks — the brand reads as something built with care.

IBM Plex Mono · Labels · Codes · Metadata
Principles
  1. Prove readiness, not certainty. Surface what you know and what you don’t.
  2. Operator-in-the-loop. Humans hold every meaningful decision.
  3. Honest escalation. Gaps in capability or access are surfaced, not hidden.
  4. Slow growth, compound returns. Patience is a feature, not a limitation.
  5. Domain-neutral architecture. The substrate is the same whether the work is marketing, ops, or strategy.
